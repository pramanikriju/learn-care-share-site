Fundraising

A storm surge of 16ft (5m) flooded the area, and over 1 million homes were damaged just in the state of West Bengal. The state government estimated that the storm caused at least ₹1 trillion ($13.2 billion) in damage and directly affected 70 percent of the state's population. State officials described the storm's effects there as worse than that of COVID-19.

We are seeking donations to provide relief to those affected by this destructive storm. All of the proceeds will go to Rotary District 3291 and the Bimalendu Ghosh Foundation. Rotary 3291 is the Kolkata branch of the Rotary International. Its goal is to provide clean water, food, and medical care to the impoverished population of West Bengal. The Bimalendu Ghosh Foundation is an India based nonprofit organization focused on helping underprivileged women and girls develop skills and escape poverty. All of the money raised will be donated to these foundations, and the money will be used to provide clean water, medical services, food, and shelter to those affected by the storm.

Teaching

Tutoring kids Grades 5-8 With our organization’s mission always in mind, we strive to find new ways to educate students during the COVID-19 pandemic. We specialize in a variety of subjects, including math, chemistry, biology, physics, Spanish, and English. These subjects are taught by high school students who are interested in these subjects and want to pursue them further. Our sessions are one-on-one so that we can tailor the curriculum based on the needs of each student. Education should be available to everyone, despite any circumstance. Therefore, our hourly prices are little to none.
